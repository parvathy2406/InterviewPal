General Data Analyst Interview Questions:

1. Explain left, right, inner, and outer joins.
2. How would you handle missing values in a dataset?
3. What is the difference between OLAP and OLTP?
4. Explain a situation where your analysis changed a decision.
5. What is a primary key and foreign key?
6. How do you ensure data quality?
7. Explain a time you worked with unstructured data.
8. What is hypothesis testing?
9. Why should we hire you?
10. Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
